### Auto-Generate Leads from Google Forms
**Tool:** Zapier
**Desc:** Automate lead generation from Google Forms to Notion for follow-up
**Prompt:** Create a Zap that triggers a new Notion page every time a new submission is made to a Google Form. The page should include the form responses and a link to the form. Blueprint: {revenue_target: 100 leads, seller_role: lead_generator}.
**Hook:** Sell lead generation services to businesses

### Auto-Tag Leads in Notion
**Tool:** Notion
**Desc:** Automate lead tagging in Notion based on form responses
**Prompt:** Create a Notion formula that automatically tags new leads based on the responses to a specific question in a Google Form. Blueprint: {revenue_target: 500 leads/month, seller_role: lead_generator}.
**Hook:** Sell lead generation services to businesses
